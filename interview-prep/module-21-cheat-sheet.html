12 Day-Before Cheat Sheet

Have ready in your head

  • One GenAI project story with metrics.
  • One transformation story (people + tech).
  • One failure story with the lesson.
  • One disagreement story you handled well.
  • Your honest view of Agentic AI in production.
  • A system design walked through end-to-end in 10 minutes.

Phrases that signal seniority

  • "What's the simpler thing that would work?"
  • "I'd want to see the eval before I trust the demo."
  • "Let's make this measurable before we scale it."
  • "I'd push back on that scope — here's why."
  • "The biggest risk isn't the model, it's the data layer / governance / change management."
Sleep, hydrate, and reread the JD an hour before the call. Map every question back to one of their listed skills.
